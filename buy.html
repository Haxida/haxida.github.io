How to Buy

1. Install MetaMask or TrustWallet

Open your Google Chrome or Firefox and visit metamask.io.
Download the MetaMask chrome extension and set up a wallet.
On mobile? Get MetaMask’s app for iPhone or Android.

2. Purchase BNB on an Exchange

Transfer BNB to your MetaMask or TrustWallet.

3. Add Binance Smart Chain to Metamask

Follow the instructions of this link.

4. Swap on Poocoin

Visit Poocoin and click connect wallet.

Copy the HAX contract address below and set your slippage to AUTO SLIPPAGE highlighting it green.
