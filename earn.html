How to provide liquidity


 

"Liquidity" is critical to increasing the value of HAX.

You can add liquidity for any pair of tokens by betting both through pancakeswap's Liquidity page.

We encourage everyone to add liquidity to the pool de HAX.

In exchange for adding liquidity, HAX will increase the value and you will receive LP Token that will earn you a percentage of the profits over trading fees for that pair when you withdraw the LP Tokens.


 

In contrast, when you withdraw the LP Token, the value of HAX will also go down.


 

Step 1: Go to PancakeSwap

Access the HAX pool on pancakeSwap by clicking on this link.  And connect to wallet in pancakeSwap.

Step 2: Set capital

Now you must decide how much capital to contribute.  Enter an amount on one of the tokens under “Input”. The other should be calculated and filled automatically.  (If one of the token does not have enough balance. You will see an error and the button being greyed out. Please enter a lower amount to proceed.).

Step 3: Contribute and Approve

Click on the CONTRIBUTE button. If you are adding liquidity against tokens other than BNB, you may need to click enable twice for each token in your pair.   Your wallet will ask you to confirm the action, the Supply button will then light up.   Click to proceed (Your wallet will ask you for confirmation. Confirm your transaction from your wallet).

Step 4: Receive your LP Token

Soon after, you will see your LP Token balance at the bottom of the page.  You can repeat the above steps to add more liquidity, or add liquidity to different pairs.

Are you still in doubt?

For more information on PancakeSwap Liquidity Pools, please visit:   How to Add/Remove Liquidity
